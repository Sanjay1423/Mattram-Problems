Gift for Birthday 1:

Penny is celebrating her 25th birthday. Her friend Leonard promised her that he will buy her a new shoes on her birthday if she solves the 
question asked by him. He asks Penny to find out whether the year on which she was born is a leap year or not. Help her to solve this puzzle 
so that she celebrates her birthday happily. If her birth year is 1995 and it is a leap year display “1995 is a leap year.” Else display “1995 
is not a leap year.”

Input Format

Input consist of 1 integer.

Constraints

NA

Output Format

Output consist of 1 string.

Sample Input 0

1995
Sample Output 0

1995 is not a leap year
